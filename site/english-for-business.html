Learn Languages On The Go!

We are a bunch of young professionals from different countries i.e. Switzerland. France, Spain, Germany, Canada travelling around the world & creating oportunities to the locals of the places we visit & teach them our languages & cultures. Not only you get the opportunity to learn a language from the natives but also get to know the culture & traditions which can help you in your future if you decide to pursue a career in any foreign countries.

We Organise Classes in three ways to make it more fun & interactive!!

1-on-1 Lessons

We can organize lessons individually organized outdoors, in designated places, restaurants for better real life experiences

Group Lessons

We can organize lessons in small groups organized outdoors like walking tours, in designated centres, cafes for better real life experiences

Remotely

Lessons can be scheduled individually or in small groups remotely at your convenient schedules on Zoom, Google meets etc.
